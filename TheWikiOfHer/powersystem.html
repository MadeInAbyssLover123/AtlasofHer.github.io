Base Power Systems - Uncatagorized

Hallmarks

Hallmarks are the stats of a Husk, defining how capable they are in different ways. You can just skip this part if you'd like.

Direct Hallmarks - Attributes to the body and mind

Brawn (BRN) - Increases physical (Like agility) and brawn ability damage, increases carry strength, increases armor penetration, and allows players to hold heavy weapons.

Vitality (VTL) - Increases HP, increases defense, increases block HP, reduces chances of being ragdolled, increases resistance against ragdolling obstacles (Eg; strong blowing wind), and increases overall survivability.

Agility (AGL) - Increases dash and slide distance, reduces slowness by any ability, increases distance of wall climbing (Can unlock “Secondary Grasp”), and increases overall speed and jump power.

Dexterity (DEX) - Increases swingrate, increases weapon unsheathing and sheathing, increases ability casting speed, increases ranged weapon accuracy, increases crit-rate and crit-damage, reduces effects of physical debuffs, and increases overall attack speed.

Intellect (INT) - Increases mana capacity and magic damage, and increases AoE ability radius by 1 stud every 4 points put in INT.

Resilience (RSL) - Resilience (RLC) - Increases sanity, increases resistance against the “Kaleido-fect,” increases resistance and mental attacks.

Charisma (CHR) - Increases NPC persuasion, gives charismatic debuffs to hit enemies, gain more dialogue options, reduces hostile detection, increases distance transparency (Unlocks as a tarot card, becomes more transparent for players the more far away you are), increases morality, increases team morale, give charisma buffs to allies passively (Unlocks at level 10 charisma), increases vocabulary level and comprehension, and overall RIZZ.

Weaponry Hallmarks

Increases overall mastery over weapons types

Light Mastery (LHT) - Increases M1 damage and overall usage of light weapons. Unlocks “LHT Techniques.” Including martial arts and pistols.

Medium Mastery (MED) - Increases M1 damage and overall usage of medium weapons. Unlocks “MED Techniques.” Including rifles.

Heavy Mastery (HVY) - Increases M1 damage and overall usage of heavy weapons. Unlocks “HVY Techniques.” Including hand cannons.

Spirit Mastery (SPT) - Increases M1 damage and overall usage of mage weapons. Unlocks “SPT Techniques.”

Bag of Goodies

In return for having enough stats in Hallmarks, goodies can be obtained!

The Bag of Goodies can be found in the Hallmarks section, at the bottom right. It will become open everytime the player levels up their Husk.
